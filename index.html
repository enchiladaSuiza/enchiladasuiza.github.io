Anime review

Yuru Yuri

YuruYuri is a super chill slice-of-life anime with a heavy emphasis on yuri and ships. It's really light-hearted and mad hilarious so that's why I personally love it. It has 3 seasons, a movie and a few OVAs, as well as a few spin-offs and music albums. Best girl is Ayano.
